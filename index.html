Our software solution aims at being simple and user friendly. It consists of simple and neat UI. It aims to sell the produce of the farmers with the help of interface between the farmer and the buyers. That would bring change in the off market. It will provide the transparency in selling/buying prices of the produce. Since our website is user friendly, anyone can easily access it which will help people in saving their time. Our website consists of profiles for both the farmers and buyers who can upload their details on the site. It will be used to make the work easier for both farmers and buyers. With the help of datasets it will generate the best price suited for the product and then the buyer can contact them offline/online on based on prediction. In this way it will benefit both the seller and the buyer. As in today's world farmers struggle in selling their produce in higher rate or to get better profitability on the produce that they have made from their hard work on the farms. The farmer's can update their produce on the portal by using simple interface using internet on their smartphone or by using simple SMS for those who do not have one. And our website would be able to read these SMS from the farmer and update the details on the web portal of the farmer. These details will appear to the buyers. And the buyer will be able to contact the seller to purchase the produce or to negotiate. In this way the farmers will be able to get a better price for their produce by letting go of all middle men and the transportation costs.
